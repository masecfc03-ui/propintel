FAQ

Common questions.

What types of properties does DealLens work on?
Any property type — strip centers, multifamily, land, SFR, industrial, office. If it has a listing URL or an address, DealLens can analyze it.
Where does the data come from?
Every data point comes from a verifiable public source: county assessor records (DCAD), county clerk deed records, TxDOT traffic data, FEMA flood maps, Census Bureau, Texas Secretary of State, city GIS, and EPA databases. Pro tier adds DataZapp skip trace. Every field in the report is hyperlinked to its source.
Are there any estimated or AI-generated numbers in the report?
No. That's the entire point. Every number is a verified public record fact. The only "estimates" are items marked clearly as sample values in the demo — live reports pull real-time data. We do not generate projected NOI, DSCR, or cap rates without a verified rent roll.
How long does a report take?
Starter reports deliver in under 60 seconds. Pro reports take 90–120 seconds due to the parcel pull and skip trace. Enterprise bulk runs are queued and delivered by email.
What format is the report?
Reports are delivered as formatted PDFs. Enterprise customers also receive JSON output via API for integration into their own systems.
Does this replace due diligence?
No. DealLens is your first-pass filter — it cuts 2–3 hours of public record research down to 60 seconds. It does not replace a full title search, Phase I ESA, rent roll review, or appraisal. We say that clearly in every report.
