FAQ

Common questions.

What types of properties does PropIntel work on?
Any property type — strip centers, multifamily, land, SFR, industrial, office. If it has a listing URL or an address, PropIntel can analyze it.
Where does the data come from?
Every data point comes from a verifiable public source: county assessor records (DCAD), county clerk deed records, TxDOT traffic data, FEMA flood maps, Census Bureau, Texas Secretary of State, city GIS, and EPA databases. Pro tier adds DataZapp skip trace. Every field in the report is hyperlinked to its source.
Are there any estimated or AI-generated numbers in the report?
Not from thin air. Every number is a verified public record fact. The Pro report's Deal Analyzer calculates DSCR and cash-on-cash from the listing's stated cap rate — clearly labeled as unverified until you have the rent roll. Live reports pull real-time data. We never fabricate NOI, cap rates, or projections.
How long does a report take?
Starter reports deliver in under 60 seconds. Pro reports take 90–120 seconds due to the parcel pull and skip trace. Enterprise bulk runs are queued and delivered by email.
What format is the report?
Reports are delivered as formatted PDFs. Enterprise customers also receive JSON output via API for integration into their own systems.
Does this replace due diligence?
No. PropIntel is your first-pass filter — it cuts 2–3 hours of public record research down to 60 seconds. It does not replace a full title search, Phase I ESA, rent roll review, or appraisal. We say that clearly in every report.
