SMS Messaging Program — Opt-In & Terms

This page describes how Mathis Land Co. uses text messaging to communicate with land owners and interested parties.

Program Description

Mathis Land Co. sends SMS text messages to landowners in Texas who may be interested in receiving a cash offer for their vacant land. Messages may include:

  • Introduction and cash offer inquiries
  • Follow-up messages regarding your property
  • Responses to your replies

Message frequency: up to 3 messages per contact. Messaging stops immediately upon reply of STOP.

How to Opt In

You may receive a text message from Mathis Land Co. if:

  • You filled out a contact form on our website requesting information
  • You replied to an outreach text and consented to further communication
  • You provided your phone number directly to Mathis Land Co.

How to Opt Out

To stop receiving messages from Mathis Land Co. at any time, simply reply:

  • STOP — to unsubscribe immediately
  • HELP — to receive support information

After texting STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Mathis Land Co. does not charge for messages sent, but your carrier's standard SMS rates may apply.

Privacy

Mathis Land Co. does not sell, rent, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your contact information is used solely to communicate with you regarding your property and potential cash offers.

Information collected may include: name, phone number, property address, and communication history. This information is stored securely and used only for the purpose of making you a cash offer for your land.
